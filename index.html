The platform

A working commercial stack built on top of CriOS Nova, Crowe Logic's chemoinformatics engine, and a 194-agent architecture specialized for psychedelic tryptamines, phenethylamines, ergolines, and related scaffolds.

Compound knowledge base

Fifteen Schedule I and adjacent compounds. Each carries PubMed-cited receptor binding data, metabolic pathways, drug interactions, toxicology, and FDA status. Queryable by target, potency, or class.

29 expert agents

Twenty-four Molecular Design specialists, three Psychedelic Specialists, and two Core orchestrators. Each one is an AI composite persona grounded in real literature. The reasoning is PhD-grade; the disclosure is honest.

Cultivation intelligence

Six Psilocybe species and six commercial P. cubensis strains with peer-reviewed alkaloid profiles. Match a target alkaloid profile to ranked cultivar and strain candidates with predicted dry-weight percentages.
